* Construction *

Base                    : ZDoomWars302_2.pk3, ZDoomWarsMaps(Various versions).wad, Confetti.wad(Which is also based off coloredmonsters.wad), and TZDwarsv6.6 all thrown into a blender and this is the result.

Editor(s) used          : Slade, Photoshop, and DoomBuilder 2 
May Not Run With...     : GZdoom, Older versions of Zandronum, Skulltag, other such fun ports
